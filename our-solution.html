Tangi Solution Overview

Breaking Down the Banking Barrier

At the heart of our solution is the most significant achievement in the cannabis payment space—securing a banking relationship. This is no small feat, as every other payment provider has struggled to gain this level of compliance and transparency with financial institutions. Major banks and payment networks such as Visa and Mastercard have long refused to service the cannabis industry due to regulatory concerns, leaving retailers vulnerable to exploitative practices and uncompliant alternatives.

We have shattered this barrier by establishing a relationship with a forward-thinking bank, a first for the industry. This bank-backed payment platform is a game-changer for cannabis retailers, offering them the opportunity to process payments securely, transparently, and most importantly, legally.

Traditional Payment Methods: Giving Retailers What They’ve Been Waiting For

Our solution is designed to be as familiar and seamless as possible for both retailers and consumers. We offer traditional payment methods, which are widely recognized and used daily in other retail sectors, integrated into one system. These methods include:

  • Credit and Debit Card Payments – The standard for payments across nearly all industries.
  • Tap-to-Pay (Contactless Payments) – A quick and secure option that's gaining popularity.
  • PIN Debit Transactions – Another widely used payment method offering security and convenience.
  • Mobile Wallet Payments (e.g., Apple Pay, Google Pay) – Enabling consumers to pay directly from their smartphones.
  • Bank Transfers – A direct and secure method for larger transactions.

These methods allow us to immediately provide cannabis retailers with the same familiar payment options they have used for years, finally eliminating the need for cash-only transactions and unreliable, non-compliant alternatives. These options give consumers the flexibility and security they expect, and help businesses operate more smoothly, while significantly reducing the risks and challenges that come with cash-only transactions.

The Token Solution: More Than Just a Payment Method

While the traditional payment methods form the foundation of our solution, the Tangi Token is the driving force behind our innovative approach to cannabis payment processing. Here’s why:

  • Lower Transaction Fees for Retailers: Traditional payment methods charge retailers higher fees, often shared with multiple parties (banks, payment processors, etc.). With the token payment system, retailers pay a lower transaction fee, saving them money.
  • Consumer Incentives: Consumers who opt for token payments are rewarded with discounts, loyalty points, and exclusive offers. These incentives not only make the token payment option more appealing to consumers but also encourage repeat business and greater customer loyalty.
  • The Most Profitable Option for Us: The token payment system provides the highest profit margins for us as a company. This enables us to reinvest into scaling and improving our services, making it more attractive for both retailers and consumers, and driving long-term growth.
  • Independence from Banking Networks: One of the most important benefits of the token solution is that it allows us to disengage from the banking system entirely. While traditional payment methods rely on third-party banks and payment processors, the token allows us to directly control the entire ecosystem, eliminating any reliance on external financial institutions and giving us complete independence.

How This Helps Retailers and Consumers

For retailers, our payment solution offers the following benefits:

  • Reduced Fees: Token payments come with lower transaction costs compared to traditional payment methods, saving businesses money that can be reinvested in growth.
  • Operational Efficiency: The seamless integration of traditional and token payments from a single terminal makes it easier for retailers to manage transactions, reducing the complexity of using multiple payment systems.
  • Increased Revenue: Token adoption drives higher profit margins for retailers, thanks to the token’s lower fees and the ability to offer discounts and loyalty programs that attract customers.
  • Greater Consumer Loyalty: By offering consumers rewards and incentives for using the token, retailers can build stronger customer relationships and increase customer retention.

For consumers, the benefits are just as compelling:

  • Familiarity and Convenience: Consumers can choose to pay using traditional methods like debit/credit cards or mobile wallets, which they are already familiar with, making adoption easy and seamless.
  • Rewards and Savings: By switching to the token, consumers can unlock exclusive discounts, loyalty points, and other incentives that make their purchasing experience more rewarding.
  • A Safe, Transparent Solution: With our compliant system, consumers can be confident that they are using a legitimate, trustworthy payment solution, free from the risks associated with unregulated alternatives.

A Complete Ecosystem Powered by the Token

The true power of our solution lies in the token, which serves as the central hub of our ecosystem. While traditional payment methods capture the consumer and get them onboard, it’s the token that drives the real value. By providing rewards, savings, and lower fees, we are incentivizing both consumers and retailers to make the switch to token payments, which will naturally lead to massive token adoption. And as the token grows in value, it will continue to drive this adoption, creating a self-sustaining cycle of demand.

In Summary: Our solution is the first compliant payment system for the cannabis industry, offering traditional payment methods and the Tangi Token. This creates an ecosystem where both retailers and consumers benefit from lower fees, rewards, and enhanced loyalty, while providing a legal and scalable payment solution. The token is the key to securing market dominance in a $33 billion industry, and we’ve designed everything with the token’s success in mind. By solving the industry's biggest pain points, we’re not just providing a service—we’re shaping the future of cannabis payments.
