Brian Cowen

FORMER PRIME MINISTER OF IRELAND

We are incredibly fortunate to have Brian Cowen, former Taoiseach (Prime Minister) of Ireland, as part of our team. With a distinguished career in politics, economics, and governance, Brian brings an unparalleled level of experience, leadership, and strategic insight to our project.

As Ireland's 11th Taoiseach (2008-2011), Brian led the country through one of the most challenging economic periods in modern history, navigating complex financial landscapes with resilience and determination. Prior to that, he served as Minister for Finance, where he played a pivotal role in shaping Ireland's economic policies. His career also includes key ministerial positions in Foreign Affairs, Health, and Transport, demonstrating his versatility and depth of expertise in managing large-scale national and international initiatives.

Having overseen high-level economic policy and financial regulation at the highest levels of government, Brian's experience is invaluable as we drive our mission forward. His insights into global financial systems, regulatory frameworks, and economic development provide us with a competitive edge as we scale our operations and cement our place as a leader in the market.

With Brian Cowen on board, we are backed by world-class expertise that will help shape our strategy, navigate regulatory landscapes, and ensure long-term success.

Ron Strauss

VISIONARY ENTREPRENEUR & CEO OF TANGI

We are privileged to have Ron Strauss leading Tangi as CEO, bringing over 20 years of experience in founding and scaling high-tech startups. As a serial entrepreneur, Ron has built and exited multiple successful companies across automation, imaging, and embedded computing industries. His proven track record of innovation, leadership, and business acumen makes him the perfect driving force behind Tangi's mission to revolutionize payment solutions in the cannabis sector.

Career Highlights & Successful Exits

Founder & CEO of Focus Automation Systems Inc. – A leader in industrial automation, Focus later spun out Mitra Imaging Inc., which was acquired by Agfa-Gevaert, and Focus Systems, which was acquired by V Technology Corporation.

Founder & CEO of Avida Systems – A major supplier of embedded computing technology for military, telecom, and transportation industries, ultimately acquired by General Electric.

Vice President & Business Development Director, General Electric – After the Avida acquisition, Ron spent five years leading GE's international embedded computing business, expanding its global footprint.

Academic & Global Business Expertise

Ron holds a Computer Systems Engineering degree and has furthered his expertise through executive training at Harvard University, the GE Jack Welch Management Training Centre, and the University of Waterloo. With international experience, including studies at Beijing Sinoland College, Ron speaks intermediate-level Mandarin Chinese, equipping him with a global business perspective that has been instrumental in securing strategic partnerships.

A Personal Mission: Advocacy & Resilience

Ron is a brain cancer survivor, and his personal journey has fueled his commitment to advancing medical cannabis accessibility. He actively supports cancer research programs and initiatives for children with epilepsy, advocating for alternative medical solutions and broader industry acceptance.

Leadership at Tangi: Bringing Compliance & Innovation to Cannabis Payments With Ron at the helm, Tangi is set to disrupt the cannabis payment industry with the first-ever bank-backed, compliant solution. His expertise in scaling businesses, securing acquisitions, and navigating complex regulatory environments ensures that Tangi is positioned for mass adoption and long-term success.

As Tangi rolls out its groundbreaking payment platform, Ron’s visionary leadership will guide the company toward dominating the cannabis payments market—offering investors an opportunity to be part of one of the most transformative financial solutions in the industry

AEROSPACE VISIONARY & BLOCKCHAIN PIONEER

We are privileged to have Stan Larimer as a key member of the Tangi leadership team, bringing a unique blend of expertise in both aerospace engineering and blockchain innovation. His deep understanding of technology and its potential to disrupt traditional industries has made him a sought-after thought leader in the space.

With a proven track record of innovation in both aerospace and blockchain, Stan brings invaluable experience, vision, and technical expertise to Tangi. Dr. Pritesh Kumar

EXPERT IN CANNABINOID PHARMACOLOGY AND CANNABIS POLICY

We are privileged to have Dr. Pritesh Kumar as a key advisor to our team. With a Ph.D. in Pharmacology and Toxicology from the University of Louisville, School of Medicine, specializing in cannabinoid pharmacology, Dr. Kumar brings a wealth of knowledge and experience to our team.

Professional Highlights
  • Managing Director of PhytoSciences GmbH: Dr. Kumar leads this Switzerland-based consultancy, which comprises over 100 scientists and analysts across Europe, Africa, Canada, India, and the USA. The firm specializes in investment due diligence, analytical testing, clinical trial solutions, novel drug delivery technology development, and commercial bioprocessing. Under his leadership, PhytoSciences has assisted start-up companies in raising over $150 million in equity and debt financing.
  • Head of Capital Markets at Achio Capital Partners: In this role, Dr. Kumar leverages his deep understanding of the legal cannabis industry, including biotechnology and pharmaceuticals, to guide investment strategies and capital market activities.
  • Chief Scientific Officer (CSO) for PharmaCielo Ltd: Dr. Kumar served as the CSO for PharmaCielo Ltd (TSXV: PCLO) (OTCQX: PCLOF), a Canadian company with operations in Colombia, South America. He was responsible for the company's R&D, global business product development strategy, and Phase I development of a state-of-the-art GMP pharmaceutical cannabis bioprocessing plant.
  • Advisor to the World Health Organization (WHO): Dr. Kumar has provided consultation services to the 40th Meeting of the Expert Committee on Drug Dependence regarding cannabis toxicology, contributing to global policy discussions on cannabis regulation.
Academic Contributions
  • Director of Cannabinoid Research at Canadore College: Dr. Kumar has served as the Director of Cannabinoid Research, advancing studies in cannabinoid pharmacology and therapeutic applications.
  • Scientific Advisor for Addis Ababa University, School of Pharmacy (SoP) in Ethiopia: In this role, he facilitates advancements in graduate student education, promotes the interests of the SoP, and develops relationships between the SoP and the private sector.
Publications and Presentations

Dr. Kumar has been published in numerous scientific journals, including the European Journal of Pharmacology, Pharmacology and Physiology, Journal of Medicinal Chemistry, Investigative Ophthalmology & Visual Science, and Biochemical and Biophysical Research Communications. He has also authored a book chapter on polymorphisms in the cannabinoid receptor 2 and related impacts on certain pathologies and has presented at several international symposiums.

Role at Tangi

Dr. Kumar's extensive experience in cannabinoid research, cannabis policy consulting, and pharmaceutical development provides our team with unparalleled expertise. His insights into laboratory facility design, analytical methodology for cannabinoid analysis, and quality assurance systems ensure that our products meet the highest standards of compliance and efficacy. His global perspective and connections within the cannabis industry enhance our ability to navigate complex regulatory landscapes and drive innovation in cannabis-based therapeutics.

Having Dr. Pritesh Kumar as part of the Tangi team underscores our commitment to scientific excellence, regulatory compliance, and the development of high-quality cannabis products that meet the needs of patients and consumers worldwide.
