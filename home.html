For investors, the question is always the same: Where’s the opportunity? What’s the edge? Why

At Tangi, we provide compliant banking and payment processing solutions to industries that major banks and card networks refuse to serve. One of the most significant opportunities today lies in the legal cannabis industry, which generated $33 billion in cash sales last year alone.

Where You Fit In — And Why This Opportunity Exists

The Problem

Payment solutions in the cannabis industry have not failed because they lack innovation. They have failed because they lack one crucial element—a banking partner.

Despite the legal cannabis industry generating $33 billion in retail sales annually, major banks and payment networks like Visa and MasterCard refuse to support it. This has forced 8,000 retailers and 60 million consumers into an outdated, cash-only system—creating inefficiencies, security risks, and lost revenue..

Without a bank to support transactions, every payment provider before us has either collapsed or been forced into non-compliance, inevitably getting shut down. That’s why the market is still dominated by cash, despite its $33 billion annual turnover.

Our Solution

Our competitive advantage isn’t patentable technology or specialized hardware. It’s the one thing that no other provider has obtained and the one thing required for success in this sector: a banking partner. We have secured a banking relationship with a forward-thinking bank that shares our vision of providing compliant banking and payment services to the cannabis industry.

This exclusive banking relationship is our most valuable asset. It’s the foundation that allows our payment system to operate legally and sustainably, and it’s what gives our token the credibility and utility to thrive.

Our all-in-one payment terminal offers fully bank-backed payment options — including debit, credit, and direct bank-to-bank transfers — giving cannabis retailers the compliance, speed, and convenience they’ve long been denied. But the real innovation is Tangi Token. While traditional methods attract and onboard the customer, every road leads to token payments. Thanks to our exclusive banking partnership, we’re the only provider able to offer this fully compliant bridge — allowing us to easily capture the market of 60 million unbanked cannabis consumers and gradually transition them to the token, where the true value lies for retailers, consumers, and investors alike.

It positions Tangi not just to participate in the market, but to dominate it. Because our payment infrastructure is built on compliance, our token is now on a direct path to adoption among up to 60 million end users who need it as a tool for everyday transactions.

Vesting Notice

All Round 2 token purchases will be subject to a linear vesting period. This decision is rooted in our commitment to protecting the integrity of the project and safeguarding the long-term value of your investment.

Why It's Good For You

Each month, your vested tokens will be automatically released in equal monthly portions, ensuring smooth distribution and full transparency. This structure promotes a measured and stable post-listing environment, reduces volatility, and supports long-term price health for all participants.

  • Price Protection – Linear vesting prevents sudden sell-offs and promotes long-term value creation.
  • Investor Fairness – Everyone exits gradually, preserving equity across the community.
  • Sustainable Growth – It allows time for adoption, end-user onboarding, and organic token utility growth.
  • Aligned Vision – It aligns all investor timelines with our rollout, service delivery, and public listing strategy.

How Vesting Works:

Buyers in the General Presale will be vested over 12 months from the date of purchase. Buyer opting to join the Elite Membership have a shorter 9-month vesting period from the date of purchase.

Understanding Your Token Release Schedule (Vesting)

When you buy tokens in the presale, they aren't sent to your wallet all at once. Instead, they are released to you gradually — this is called vesting.

Standard Presale Buyers: As an example, if you buy 48,000 tokens, you'll receive 4,000 tokens per month for 12 months. This monthly release happens automatically, and the same rule applies to any amount you buy — unless you qualify as an Elite Member (see below).

Elite Members: If you buy 50,000 tokens or more, you become part of our Elite Membership group. Elite Members receive their tokens over 9 months. For example, if you purchase 90,000 tokens, you'll receive 10,000 tokens per month for 9 months.
