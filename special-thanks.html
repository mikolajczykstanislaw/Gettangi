Special Thanks to Round 1 Investors

We want to take a moment to offer our deepest thanks to the 2,500 investors who have been with us from the very beginning. Your unwavering support, patience, and commitment have been nothing short of extraordinary, and it is because of you that we are where we are today.

When we started, we were no more than a concept—a vision of what could be. You didn’t just invest capital; you believed in our mission, in our potential, and in the people behind the project. You backed us when we had no proof of success, and you absorbed the initial risks, knowing full well that there were no guarantees.

There was no blueprint for what we were trying to achieve, and when we pivoted, it wasn’t easy. We had to change our strategy at a pivotal time, and you stood by us. We know it was a challenging moment. It was a risk for you as investors, and yet you stayed patient, understanding, and supportive. You showed incredible flexibility and tolerance when we were forced to throw a curveball and stall while we redefined the future direction of the project.

Throughout it all, you held us to account. You kept us grounded and committed to our mission. You raised valid questions, voiced concerns, and shared your perspectives—always with the best interests of the project and fellow investors in mind. The level of integrity and accountability you’ve brought to this journey has been invaluable.

You’ve also supported the newest members of the community—offering a steady hand and encouragement to those who joined after you. You’ve understood that we’re all in this together, and your continued support for the new round of investors has been instrumental in fostering a strong, thriving community.

We acknowledge that you endured significant time loss while your capital was tied up during this early phase of the project. But despite the waiting, you stood firm. You believed in us, and your belief has been rewarded with the progress we’ve made so far.

We are truly fortunate to have had you by our side, and we want you to know that every success we achieve from here on out is built on the foundation you helped us lay. The future is bright, and it’s thanks to you that we’ve been able to overcome the many obstacles we’ve faced.

As we move forward, know that your support has been vital in making this project what it is today. From the bottom of our hearts, thank you. We will continue to work tirelessly to ensure that your trust in us is well-placed and that we deliver the success you’ve so rightly earned.

Here’s to the journey ahead, and to you—the incredible Round 1 investors who made it all possible.
