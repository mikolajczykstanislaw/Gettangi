The Potential ROI

From The Investors Perspective

As an investor, your focus is simple: How does this token increase in value, and how do you exit profitably?

Tangi Token isn’t a speculative gamble—it’s a first-to-market, utility-driven payment solution with built-in demand. Every step of our rollout is designed to drive adoption, create buy pressure, and ensure investor profits.

Everyone in the chain—consumers, retailers, and our company—is incentivised to adopt and promote token payments, which in turn means they unknowingly work to drive investor profits.

Here’s how we make it all happen for you:

1 Our Closed-Loop System

Creating Initial Token Adoption

Once consumers have transitioned to regular token use, we make the process effortless by setting them up on automatic replenishment. Their token wallets are topped up on a recurring basis, ensuring they always have tokens available for purchases. This seamless system means consumers continue enjoying rewards and discounts without any extra effort, while retailers benefit from a steady flow of token transactions. The result? A self-sustaining cycle of continuous token demand, driving value and long-term growth.

2 Seamless Transition to Public Exchange

From Our Closed Loop to Investor-Driven Supply

As adoption increases and end users become reliant on the token, we gradually transition them from the closed-loop system to the public exchange—seamlessly and without disruption. As end users shift from our internal system to the public exchange, investors take over as the exclusive source of token supply—driving sustained demand and value appreciation. Since they’re already on an automatic monthly subscription with us, their wallets top up effortlessly. The only change? Instead of purchasing from our closed-loop system, their auto-renewal shifts to the exchange, ensuring continuous demand without them even noticing the transition. This means millions of buyers will be looking for tokens, repeatedly purchasing every week, and the only people selling are early investors like you.

3 Presale Investors Control Supply

Demand Will Drive the Value

At the time of public listing, the entire circulating supply—just 50 million presale tokens—will be exclusively held by you and your fellow early investors, feeding the anticipated surge in demand.

New investors and millions of end users will be competing to buy, but they can only purchase from you. With fixed supply and surging demand, the token’s value is poised for natural appreciation.

  • Built-in, predictable buy pressure
  • Not just one-time buyers—every user is on repeat purchase
  • Millions of end users automatically replenishing their wallets every month

As a Round 2 investor, you’re in a prime position to capitalize on continuous demand—whether selling to incoming investors at listing or holding as mass adoption accelerates.

4 Potential Token Value

The Numbers Speak for Themselves

Let's look at the numbers based on potential market penetration:

  • At just 1% of the market ($330M in transactions), each token would be worth $6.60
  • At 5% market penetration ($1.65B in transactions), each token could be $33+
  • Capture 10% of the market, and now you’re looking at a $66+ per token value.

5 Multiple Exit Strategies

Sell When It Makes Sense for You

Exit Immediately at Public Listing

Upon public listing, a new wave of investors will be drawn to the project's credibility, recognizing the early-mover advantage before mass adoption takes hold. They will see the opportunity to become the supply to an inevitable surge in demand from end users—driving token appreciation as fixed supply meets overwhelming demand. As more retailers adopt our payment solution and millions of consumers integrate the token into their daily transactions, the value continues to rise, attracting yet another wave of investors. For Round 2 presale investors, this presents a prime exit strategy—selling into a rapidly growing market at a premium.

OR

Capitalize on Long-Term Consumer Adoption

For investors seeking maximum returns, holding onto tokens until consumer adoption peaks presents a lucrative alternative. As we scale, retailers will continue onboarding, each bringing thousands of new repeat buyers into the ecosystem. These consumers will rely on the token for transactions, creating sustained buy pressure on the exchange. This is not a one-time pump—it’s an ongoing, compounding demand cycle. With a fixed supply of just 50 million tokens in circulation, scarcity will drive value higher over time. By selling strategically as demand surges, investors can capitalize on the token’s natural appreciation as millions of end users flood the exchange in search of supply.

Sit Back and Watch It All Unfold

Tangi Token isn’t just another investment—it’s a first-mover opportunity in a $33B industry that has no compliant solution until now. The only question is, will you be holding the supply when millions of consumers come looking?
