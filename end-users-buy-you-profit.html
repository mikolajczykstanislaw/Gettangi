End Users Buy, You Profit

Where Every Transaction Builds Investor Wealth

Everyone in the chain—consumers, retailers, and our company—is incentivized to adopt, promote, and use token payments.

Retailers

Reduced Fees

Token payments have the Token payments have the lowest transaction fees across all payment methods on our terminals, saving tens of thousands of dollars annually that can be reinvested in the business's growth, while also ensuring full regulatory compliance.

Operational Efficiency

The seamless integration of traditional and token payments from a single terminal makes it easier for retailers to manage transactions, reducing the complexity of using multiple payment systems.


Additional Benefits for Retailers

  • Eliminates Cash-Handling Risks – Token payments remove the need for large cash transactions, reducing the risk of theft, robbery, and internal fraud for retailers.
  • Increased Revenue: Token adoption drives higher profit margins for retailers, thanks to the token’s lower fees and the ability to offer discounts and loyalty programs that attract customers.
  • Greater Consumer Loyalty: By offering consumers rewards and incentives for using the token, retailers can build stronger customer relationships and increase customer retention.
  • Streamlined Bookkeeping & Accounting – Digital transactions automatically record sales data, making it easier to track revenue, file taxes, and manage financial records without manual cash reconciliation.
  • Simplifies Payroll & Expense Management – With funds processed digitally, business owners can easily allocate salaries, pay suppliers, and manage operational expenses without dealing with cash logistics.
  • Real-Time Inventory & Sales Tracking – Token payments seamlessly integrate with POS systems, providing retailers with accurate, real-time insights into inventory levels, sales trends, and business performance.
  • Compliance – Eliminating cash simplifies financial audits, improves regulatory compliance, and ensures that businesses operate with full transparency, avoiding issues related to cash-heavy operations.

Consumers

Rewards and Savings

When paying with token, consumers can unlock exclusive discounts, elite benefits, loyalty points, incentives and a custom-built rewards program, enhancing their shopping experience.

Familiarity and Convenience

Consumers can choose to pay using mobile wallets, The Tangi App or the Tangi Tap & Go card which mirror payment methods they are already familiar with in every other retail sector, making adoption easy and seamless.

A Safe, Transparent Solution

With our compliant system consumers can be confident that they are using a legitimate, trustworthy payment solution, free from the risks associated with unregulated alternatives.

Our Company

Increased Profits

Of all our services, we earn the highest profit margins from token transactions, driving revenue growth, and enabling complete control over the ecosystem. This allows us to scale quickly, free from banking system limitations.

Independence from Banking Networks

One of the most important benefits of the token solution is that it allows us to disengage from the banking system entirely. While traditional payment methods rely on third-party banks and payment processors, the token allows us to directly control the entire ecosystem, eliminating any reliance on external financial institutions and giving us complete independence.
