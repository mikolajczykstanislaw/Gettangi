Tangi Tokenomics: The Economic Foundation of the Project

1. Limited Token Supply

Total Supply: 250 million tokens

Initial Circulating Supply: 50 million tokens (all held by presale investors like you)

Why it Matters: The limited supply of 50 million tokens gives the token inherent scarcity. As the market adopts the token, the increasing demand for it will create upward pressure on its value. The scarcity factor is crucial for driving the token's value.

2. Token Distribution & Ownership

Presale Investors: All 50 million tokens that will be in circulation at the time of public listing will be owned by investors who purchased during Round 1 and Round 2. These investors, including you, will control the initial supply when the token hits the public exchange.

Why it Matters: Since investors will control the initial supply, they can influence the price based on market demand, creating substantial growth potential.

3. Demand Drivers: Market Need & Token Utility

The Token's Role in Payment: The Tangi token is at the heart of a payment solution that addresses a $33 billion market. It provides a compliant and legal alternative to the current cash-only system and illegal payment solutions in the cannabis industry.

Consumer Adoption: Consumers will be incentivized to use the token due to rewards such as discounts, loyalty points, and exclusive offers. As customers gravitate towards token payments due to the compelling rewards, retailers will increasingly support and push for the token to be used.

Retailer Adoption: Retailers will benefit from token payments through significantly lower transaction fees compared to non-compliant payment methods. The token will provide them with a cheaper, faster, and more profitable payment method, driving its adoption.

4. Utility & Real-World Application

A Fully Integrated Solution: Tangi Token will operate seamlessly from our bank-integrated platform and become the preferred method due to its compliance, rewards and cost-saving benefits

Incentive-Based Adoption: Both retailers and consumers will be incentivized to adopt the token due to its economic advantages. Retailers save on transaction fees while benefiting from a loyalty program, and consumers will enjoy discounts and exclusive rewards when using the token.

5. Future Market Demand

End User Base: With an estimated 60 million potential end users and 8,000 cannabis retailers needing a compliant payment solution, the demand for Tangi tokens is poised to be substantial. The growing consumer base for cannabis-related payments will directly fuel token demand.

Real-World Use: Unlike many other speculative tokens, the Tangi token will have real-world utility, with immediate use in an established and growing market. The demand for Tangi tokens won’t just be speculative—it will be driven by consumers and retailers who will need the token for everyday transactions in the cannabis space.

6. The Path to Mass Adoption

Early Stage to Market Domination: Tangi has already solved the hardest challenge in the cannabis space: gaining a banking partner.

Mass Token Adoption Strategy: Consumers will naturally transition from cash to token payments due to the added benefits. This creates an organic path to widespread token adoption, driving massive growth potential and value appreciation.

7. ROI Potential: A Token with Real Growth

Token Value Increase: With an initial market capitalization driven by limited supply and the massive potential demand from 60 million end users, the value of Tangi token will rise as more users and retailers join the ecosystem.

Early Investment Advantage: Investors in the presale will hold 100% of the initial circulating tokens, giving them exclusive control over supply. As adoption ramps up, the investor's ability to sell their tokens to a ready, growing market will provide the opportunity for significant ROI when the token lists publicly.

Why This Matters to Investors:

By investing in Tangi, you are not just buying into a token; you’re investing in a real-world solution to an industry desperate for change. You’re buying into the first compliant payment system that not only meets a massive market need but also has an exclusive, scarce token that is built to be the cornerstone of this solution.

The Tangi token is set to revolutionize how payments are processed in the cannabis industry, and early investors have the chance to benefit from a highly profitable growth trajectory. With limited token supply, massive adoption potential, and real-world utility, Tangi’s tokenomics offer you the opportunity to capitalize on a first-mover advantage in a multi-billion-dollar industry.
